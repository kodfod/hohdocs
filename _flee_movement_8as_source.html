1 /*
2 Here's how this works:
3 
4 1. Raycast towards X sides with a certain length (test-distance) - up, down, left, right.
5 2. Find the distances of each point towards the player.
6 3. Eliminate the directions that makes us the closest to the player. (dot-scale)
7 4. If there's a spot available that is hidden from player's line of sight, prefer that.
8 5. Otherwise, prefer the direction that makes us the furthest away from the player.
9 6. Move towards the direction, making sure not to get stuck by automatically recalculating after standing still for 200ms.
10 7. Stop moving when reaching certain distance near target point, and find new point. (stop-distance)
11 */
